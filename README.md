# # Customer-Insights-And-Sales-Analysis-Excel-Python-Sql-Power-BI

This project analyses customer purchasing behaviour using **Python, SQL Server, and Power BI** to uncover trends, patterns, and key insights that support data-driven decision-making. It provides a comprehensive view of customer demographics, subscription patterns, sales performance, and product preferences.

## 🔍 Project Objectives

* Analyse customer purchasing behaviour and spending patterns
* Identify trends in subscription status and customer engagement
* Evaluate sales and revenue across product categories
* Understand the impact of demographics (age, gender) on purchasing decisions

## ⚙️ Data Processing Workflow

* Cleaned and transformed raw data using **Python (Pandas)**
* Performed feature engineering to create meaningful variables (e.g. age groups, customer segments)
* Loaded processed data into **SQL Server** for structured storage
* Connected **Power BI** to SQL Server for data modelling and visualization

## 📊 Key Features

* Interactive Power BI dashboard with KPI tracking
* Customer segmentation by age group, gender, and subscription status
* Revenue and sales analysis by category
* Subscription distribution and customer engagement insights
* Dynamic filters for detailed data exploration

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Python** (Data Cleaning, Feature Engineering, Data Processing)
* **Pandas, NumPy** (Data manipulation and transformation)
* **SQL Server** (Database storage and querying)
* **Power BI** (Data Modelling, DAX, Interactive Dashboards)
* **Excel** (Initial data preparation)

## 💡 Key Insights

* Majority of customers are non-subscribers (**~73%**), highlighting a strong opportunity for targeted subscription conversion strategies
* Clothing category generates the highest revenue and sales volume
* Young adults contribute the largest share of total revenue
* Customer purchasing behaviour varies significantly across product categories and demographic groups

## 🎯 Business Impact

* Enables businesses to better understand customer segments and improve targeting strategies
* Supports data-driven marketing decisions to increase subscription uptake
* Helps optimise product offerings based on customer demand patterns
* Drives improved decision-making through clear and actionable insights
